Academic Profile

Dr. Ammar Ibrahim is a cybersecurity researcher and lecturer specializing in blockchain, IoT security, and wireless communications. With a Ph.D. from the Military Technical College in Cairo, he has developed innovative solutions at the intersection of cybersecurity and emerging technologies.

My research integrates blockchain, IoT, and threat intelligence with practical deployment and instructional experience in secure communications.

Currently serving as a lecturer at multiple prestigious institutions including the Military Technical College and AASTMT, Dr. Ibrahim supervises undergraduate projects in blockchain security, healthcare IoT, and secure wireless communication systems.
