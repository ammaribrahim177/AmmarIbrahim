Academic Profile

Dr. Ammar Ibrahim is a researcher and lecturer specializing in cybersecurity, wireless networks, and blockchain technology. With a Ph.D. from the Military Technical College in Cairo, he has developed innovative solutions at the intersection of cybersecurity and emerging technologies.

My research integrates blockchain, IoT, and threat intelligence with practical deployment and instructional experience in secure communications.
